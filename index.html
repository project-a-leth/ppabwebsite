Mission Statement

Petals, Paws & Beans is a cute little coffee shop on the west side of Lethbridge where you can bring your dog and still gather and feel like part of the family. Sharing the same location as our flower shop, New Hope YQL, the two bays share a joining wall with entrances allowing access to either side. We also want to include additional health conscious beverage options. We strive to see our customers happy and leaving with a smile.
